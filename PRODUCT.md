# Product

## Register

product

## Users
Clinicians and doctors operating in local clinics or hospital settings who need to document patient encounters quickly and privately during or immediately after visits.

## Product Purpose
To capture, transcribe, compile, and audit clinical SOAP notes locally on-device without exposing sensitive patient health information (PHI) to the cloud or external networks.

## Brand Personality
- Editorial
- Trusted
- Clean

## Anti-references
- SaaS-cream dashboards
- Neon gradients or glowing primary buttons
- Heavy drop shadows and card-heavy grids
- Floating action buttons (FABs) and decorative glassmorphism

## Design Principles
1. **Clinical Trust through Editorial Clarity**: Emphasize clean, readable serif headings and lightweight sans-serif body text that feels like a trusted medical sheet.
2. **Local-First Confidence**: Visual indicators should reinforce that processing is occurring securely on-device with clear local status diagnostics.
3. **Restrained Utility**: UI elements should serve a clear workflow purpose rather than being decorative. Maintain strict padding, aligned layouts, and soft color accents.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- High contrast clinical colors (Forest Ink text on Linen White backgrounds).
- Large, legible tap targets for busy doctors.
- Clear error messages and programmatic guidelines for complete documentation.
